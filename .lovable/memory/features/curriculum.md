## Beginner (Koyon Baki)
1. Koyon Baki
2. Koyon Karatu da Rubutun Arabic

## Basic 1
1. Tauhid Muqarrar 1–3
2. Fiqhu Sabilul Muyassar
3. Sira Khulasatul Nurul Yaqin
4. Qur'an (5 Hizb)
5. Hadith Haqqun Mubin
6. Azkar Hisnul Muslim
7. Arabiyya
8. Durusul Arabiyya
9. Tajwid (Kitabu Tajwid)
10. Imlai

## Basic 2
1. Tauhid Muqarrar 4–6
2. Fiqhu Akhdari
3. Sira Khulasa Nurul Yaqin 2
4. Qur'an (Ankabut)
5. Hadith Bugya
6. Tahzib
7. Zadul Muslima
8. Tajwid (Hidayatul Mustafida)
9. Nahwul Wadiy 1
10. Durusul Arabiyya 2
11. Ilmul Mustalahul Hadith

## Intermediate
1. Kitab Tauhid
2. Fiqhu Iziya / Risala
3. Sira Khulasa 3
4. Qur'an (Arba'in down)
5. Tajwid (Shatibiyya)
6. Hadith (Umdah)
7. Nahwul Wadiy 2–3
8. Ulumul Qur'an

## Advanced
1. Bulugh
2. Riyadus Salihin
3. Minhajul Muslim
4. Adabul Talibil Ilm
5. Balagatul Wadiha

## Tahfiz
1. Ulumul Qur'an
2. Kaifa Tahfizul Qur'an
3. Tafsir Ibn Kathir

## General Class
1. Al-Wasiyyatul Shariyya
2. Fannut Ta'amul
3. Nisau Ahlul Jannah
4. Nisau Ahlun Nar
5. Azkar Imam Nawawi
6. Tibbun Nabawi
